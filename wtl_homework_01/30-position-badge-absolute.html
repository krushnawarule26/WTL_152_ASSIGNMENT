NEW
This box has a badge positioned in the top-right corner using position: absolute. The badge is positioned relative to its nearest positioned ancestor (the container).
SALE
Here's another box with a different badge. Notice how the badge always appears in the same position relative to the container, regardless of the content.

Key points about position: absolute:

  • The element is removed from the normal document flow
  • It's positioned relative to its nearest positioned ancestor
  • Use top, right, bottom, left properties to position it
  • Commonly used for badges, notifications, and overlays
