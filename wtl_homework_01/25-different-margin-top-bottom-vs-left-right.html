This box has 20px margin on top/bottom and 50px margin on left/right. Notice how it has more horizontal space than vertical space.
Another box with equal margins for comparison.

The syntax margin: 20px 50px means:

  • 20px margin on top and bottom
  • 50px margin on left and right

This creates a box that's more spread out horizontally than vertically.
